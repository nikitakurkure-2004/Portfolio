Derma Care Website (2022)

Purpose: The primary purpose of the Derma Care Website is to provide personalized skincare recommendations by analyzing a user’s skin type and specific skin concerns. The system aims to help users make informed skincare choices without relying solely on trial-and-error methods or expensive consultations.

Use:

  • Assists users in identifying suitable skincare products based on their skin condition
  • Reduces the risk of using incorrect or harmful skincare products
  • Acts as a digital guide for individuals seeking basic dermatological advice
  • Improves user awareness about proper skincare routines
  • Provides a simple and accessible platform for skincare consultation

Technologies Used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL

Duration: 3 Months

Team Members: 1

Blockchain-Based Online Voting System (2025)

Purpose: The primary purpose of the Blockchain-Based Online Voting System is to provide a secure, transparent, and tamper-proof digital voting platform. The system eliminates vote manipulation by storing votes on a blockchain, ensuring trust, integrity, and immutability in the election process.

Use:

  • Enables secure online voting using blockchain technology
  • Ensures vote transparency and prevents data tampering
  • Provides OTP-based authentication for voter verification
  • Uses smart contracts to automate vote recording
  • Allows real-time and verifiable election results

Technologies Used: React.js, Node.js, Solidity, Web3.js, MetaMask

Duration: 2 Years (Final Year Project)

Team Members: 4
